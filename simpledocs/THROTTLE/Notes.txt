PINOUT
-------
1 -> 9
2 -> 4
3 -> 4
4 -> GND
5 -> ~CS (see RPI pin allocation)
6 -> CLK (see RPI pin allocation)
7 -> SDI (see RPI pin allocation)
8 -> SDO (see RPI pin allocation)
9 -> +VOLTAGE
10 -> THROTTLE

COMMANDS
---------
Send one byte to set wiper position.

SPI SETUP
----------
CLK rising edge
CLK high OK
MSB first

NOTES
------
Voltage at wiper (pin 10)
	Vw = D/256 * +V
